# Introduction

I found these letters on a yellow legal pad wedged behind my desk. The pad was crumpled at the corners and covered in bite marks. I unfolded it and started reading, and I sat on the floor for forty minutes.

I asked everyone. My wife, the kids, my twin brother Nik. Nobody had been in my office. Nobody had access to that desk. The only creature who was in that room every day, without exception, while I sat at my computer and worked, was Nando. My five-pound Maltese.

The strange part is not just what the letters say. It is that they are written from Nando to Jarvis, my brother's dog. Reading them and realizing that, seeing their names, knowing these two animals, was the part I could not get past.

I am not claiming my dog wrote these letters. I am saying I cannot figure out who else did. Draw your own conclusions.
