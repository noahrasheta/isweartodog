# Task: Create Nando's canonical voice document

## What To Do

Create a comprehensive voice document that defines exactly how Nando writes. This is the single most important creative artifact in the project — it anchors every letter and prevents voice drift across writing sessions. Include: sentence length range, verbal tics and recurring phrases, irony register (cosmic seriousness that never acknowledges its own absurdity), how Nando refers to humans (with what mixture of affection and condescension), vocabulary constraints, tone markers, and 2-3 calibration paragraphs (worked examples of Nando's prose that demonstrate the voice in action). Also include a comprehensive forbidden-phrases list to prevent AI slop tells.

## Why It Matters

Voice consistency across 9 letters is the single highest-risk item in the project. Without this document, each letter risks sounding like it was written by a different author. The voice document is fed to every writing session and every editorial pass.

## Size

**Estimate:** medium

Requires creative synthesis from the research plus Noah's input on Nando's character. The calibration paragraphs are the most demanding part — they must be good enough to serve as voice anchors.

## Done When

- [ ] Sentence pattern rules defined (length range, structure preferences, rhythm)
- [ ] Verbal tics and recurring phrases catalogued (things Nando says consistently)
- [ ] Irony register specified (how Nando's cosmic seriousness works, what he never acknowledges)
- [ ] Forbidden phrases list comprehensive (AI slop tells: moreover, furthermore, tapestry, multifaceted, in essence, etc.)
- [ ] 2-3 calibration paragraphs written that demonstrate Nando's voice in action
- [ ] Noah has reviewed and approved the voice document
