# Task: Build ScrollProgress bar

## What To Do

Create the ScrollProgress Client Component — a thin progress bar at the top of the viewport that fills as the reader scrolls through the page. Use Framer Motion's useScroll hook with a scaleX transform for smooth, performant animation. Style with antique gold color to match the theme. Keep it minimal — a thin line, not a chunky bar.

## Why It Matters

Gives readers a sense of how much story remains — especially important for a long scroll that might otherwise feel endless. A small quality-of-life feature that signals craftsmanship.

## Size

**Estimate:** small

One small component with a well-documented pattern (useScroll + scaleX).

## Done When

- [ ] Thin progress bar fixed at top of viewport
- [ ] Uses useScroll + useTransform + scaleX from Framer Motion
- [ ] Styled with antique gold, thin and minimal
- [ ] Fills smoothly as user scrolls through the page
- [ ] Doesn't interfere with any other fixed elements

Project scaffolded (this component is independent of Letter content).
