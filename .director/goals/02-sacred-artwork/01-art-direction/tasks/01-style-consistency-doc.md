## What To Do

Create a comprehensive style guide that governs all 9 artwork pieces. Include: global mandate (serious sacred art, dogs as divine figures, dark backgrounds), character anchor descriptions for Nando and Jarvis (physical traits, how they should appear across different art styles), shared negative prompt block (what to exclude from every image — cute/cartoonish aesthetics, bright backgrounds, modern elements), technical specs (4:3 aspect ratio, 1600-2000px wide, dark/deep backgrounds), color palette constraints (warm tones that complement cream text on charcoal), and a per-letter art period guide mapping each letter to a specific sacred art style with named painter references (e.g., "Letter 3: Illuminated manuscript, style of the Book of Kells" not just "medieval").

Without this document, 9 images generated across multiple sessions will drift in palette, style, and character portrayal. The style guide is to artwork what the voice document is to writing — the anchor that keeps everything cohesive.
