# Gameplan

## Overview

I Swear to Dog is a cinematic storytelling website — 9 letters from a five-pound Maltese to his nephew, framed as a satirical origin mythology with a simulation hypothesis twist. Content comes first (writing, then artwork), followed by the website build and launch. The story is the soul; the site is the frame.

## Goals

1. **Goal 1: The story is written, polished, and ready to publish** -- All 9 letters, frame narrative, and editorial pipeline complete
2. **Goal 2: Every letter has a piece of sacred artwork ready to display** -- Art direction, image generation, and curation complete
3. **Goal 3: Readers experience the full story as an immersive, cinematic scroll** -- Website built, content integrated, accessible and performant
4. **Goal 4: The site is live at isweartodog.com and ready to share** -- Deployed, social cards ready, public repo clean

## Current Focus

**All goals complete.** The site is live at isweartodog.com.
